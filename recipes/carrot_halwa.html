Carrot Halwa

Description

Carrot halwa or gajar halwa is a traditional dessert made by slow cooking carrots in milk.

This recipe will help you make an incredible carrot halwa, perfect to serve at a wedding or party.

Ingredients

  • Carrots
  • Ghee
  • Milk

Steps

  1. Finely chop the carrots
  2. Cook the carrots over a medium heat for 2 hours, along with the milk and ghee
  3. Garnish with almonds, and serve with a scoop of vanilla ice cream.
