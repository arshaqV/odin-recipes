Poutine

Description

The greatest dish ever created, the French-Canadian poutine.

Few other comfort foods come close. Make this delicious dish today!

Ingredients

  • Potatoes
  • Cheese curds
  • Gravy

Steps

  1. Chop the potatoes into french fries
  2. Fry the cut potatoes, and put it in a bowl with gravy and cheese curds
  3. Drizzle more gravy generously, and serve.
